Getting There from Budapest

The venue is about a 40 minutes drive from Budapest. There is free parking without limitations on-site. If you are coming by car, please see the directions provided by the venue.

For people without cars, we'll organize shared transport for people from Kelenföld train station, leaving at 14:00. We are also planning a return trip at 02:00 AM. Please let us know in the RSVP form if you'd like to take part in the transport either way, or if the 02:00 AM time would not work for you. We'll try to accomodate as many people as we can.

Taxis are available to-and-from Budapest, altough they may cost up to £45, so if you are interested in this option its best to carpool.

Accommodation

Unfortunately we can't guarantee accomodation on-site for guests. For this reason, an option is to find a place to stay in Budapest.

You may also consider getting accomodation in one of the adjacent towns such as Etyek, which is 20 mins away by car and may be easier to organise your own travel to and from. Furthermore, Etyek has lovely vineyards, which you may be interested to check out before/after the wedding!
